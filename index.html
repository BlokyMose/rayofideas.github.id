Bio

Aspiring Game Designer

I'm a proud Christian boy, from Indonesia, and currently 20 years old. While studying a field of digital contents in Tokyo, Japan, I'm also exploring many realms of studies, such as theology, philosophy, story-writing, graphic design, sketching, programming,and also a bit of audio and music. All of that will end up in my main study, namely Game Design. It's my dream and goal to develop a game that can reach human's heart and transform it back to its purest form that once we had.

My Slogan

For 2020

At least for this year, I'll try to pursue the invention of cutting edge innovation both in technology and art. I won't only study something that is alien to me, but also challenge myself to bring something novel in my works. In other words, this year's ultimate keyword for me is "New".
