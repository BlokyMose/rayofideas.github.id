What's Beauty?

It's ugliness from another view

Photography has a bad image in the society for it sometimes decieves people to think a hideous object as a pretty one. On the contrary, photography extracts beauty from the object so it may be consumed by our eyes more easily. In other words, every object has an intrinsic beauty that if you're not keen enough to look, you'll miss it entirely.
