What's Life?

It's a book of experiences

Different from any other medium, games offer the best way to experience thrilling moments in life by providing "interactivity". Not only entertaining, games can also give multiple perspective to the player to enlighthen their way of thinking and behaving in their daily struggles in life. Though gaming is my primary interest, as you can see, I haven't finished producing one single game :(

Match Cards

Genre : Puzzle, education
Platform : Smartphones (Android & iOS)
Start Development : March, 2020
Status : Demo Version, halted
Launch : tbd
Framework : Flutter
Desc : Match Cards is designed specifically to: enhance player's ability to spot and match words, to increase player's english vocabulary, as well as other skills, such as mathematics and even japanese. The game is carefully crafted to improve user experience by charming user interface.
Dev's Note : As my first attempt to use Flutter to make a mobile app, I'm pretty proud of myself. I've received some feedback regarding the gameplay and the usage of color in the UI. There's still so much to improve before launching this app, especially because I'm bewildered by Flutter's concept of "inherited widget". ugh

T'ANDOK

Genre : Platformer, adventure
Platform : PC
Start Development : December, 2019
Status : In develpment
Launch : tbd
Framework : Unity
Desc : T'ANDOK tells a story of a dying creature that lives after mankind has gone from the face of the earth. As the dying creature, the player must explore the realm of the dead and the living continuously throughout the game to unlock the secret of immortality and power to rule the darken earth.
Dev's Note : Started with an idea to make a very small platformer which actually I successfully made, I decided to take the development and the story even further. Despite having a very simple gameplay, I manage to find a way to make T'ANDOK one of the most story-complex game I've ever made (I only made four games so far..). Hopefully it will be done by 50% by the end of this year. However, since there are a lot of design and drawing to do, it seems like launching this game is just a mere dream for now.

Mr.Clues and The Lost Pen

Genre : Detective, click-adventure
Platform : PC
Start Development : August, 2019
Status : In develpment
Launch : tbd
Framework : Unity
Desc : Mr. Clues just moved in to the City-by-the-Sea to start his real job as a true private detective. Equipped with his experience as a janitor for two years, his first case is to find the most precious pen of one of the most famous pen maker in town, Mrs. Inkwell. Under six hours, the pen must be found before the Inkwells catch their flight. With no clear motivation of why someone wants to steal a pen that has no worth what so ever, will Mr. Clues find it?
Dev's Note : I thought the concept of the game was pretty simple: click some items, find a clue and strands, deduce the who dunnit. Yet, as I dig into the characters who are involved in the case, the story gets even interesting with each character has their own inner desire and motivation. Well, you'll have to wait for this one. It's going to be hillarious.
