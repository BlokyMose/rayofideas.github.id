Risen Christ

Majestic Resurrection

In the morning, Mary Magdalene went to the tomb which is located in a garden. She was looking for Jesus, but then she only found the gardener. She wept, and asked the gardener where her Lord is. The gardener said with that sweet sound, "Mary." The gardener was Jesus, God himself, the gardener of Eden. He knows his flock, and his flock knows him. He died like a seed planted in the land of man, and now he is risen.
